Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We Have Answers

Everything you need to know about loans, insurance, and our services. Can't find your answer here? Call us directly — we're always happy to help.

What services does Bhaarat Capital offer?+
Bhaarat Capital is a one-stop financial services company based in Bisrakh, Noida. We offer Home Loans, Personal Loans, Car Loans, Business Loans, Gold Loans, Loan Against Property, Health Insurance, Life Insurance, Motor Insurance, Corporate Insurance, Property Insurance, and Mutual Fund investments. We work with 20+ banks and 15+ insurance companies to find you the best products at the best rates — all for free.
How quickly can I get my loan approved and disbursed?+
Timelines vary by loan type: Personal Loans can be approved and disbursed within 24–48 hours for eligible salaried applicants. Car Loans take 24–48 hours. Home Loans take 7–10 working days including property verification. Business Loans take 3–7 days depending on documentation. Gold Loans can be processed in as little as 30 minutes. We work with multiple lenders simultaneously to maximise speed without compromising on rate.
What CIBIL score is needed to get a loan?+
A CIBIL score of 750+ gives you access to the best interest rates and highest loan amounts. Scores between 650–749 are still eligible for most products but may carry slightly higher rates. We also work with lenders who consider applicants with lower scores — especially for gold loans and secured loans. We offer a completely free CIBIL check as part of every consultation. Even with a low score, speak to us first.
Do you charge any fees for your services?+
No. Our initial consultation, eligibility check, rate comparison, and advisory services are completely free of charge. We do not charge any upfront fees. Our income comes from banks and insurance companies after successful product issuance. The only fees you ever pay are standard bank processing fees — which we always disclose clearly upfront, with no surprises.
My loan application was rejected by my bank. Can you still help?+
Absolutely! Rejection by one bank does not mean you cannot get a loan. Different banks have different credit policies, income criteria, and risk appetite. We analyze the exact reason for rejection, suggest improvements to your application, and approach the most suitable lender from our network of 20+ banks. Many of our most successful cases started as rejections elsewhere. Call us — we will find a solution.
How do I choose the right health insurance plan for my family?+
Key factors: sum insured (minimum ₹5 Lakh for family of 4), number of network hospitals, claim settlement ratio (aim for 95%+), waiting period for pre-existing conditions, sub-limits on room rent, and co-payment clauses. Our advisors conduct a personalized needs analysis — considering your age, family size, health conditions, and budget — and recommend the genuinely best plan, not the most expensive one. This service is completely free.
Do you offer doorstep service for document collection?+
Yes, absolutely! We offer free doorstep document pickup across Noida, Greater Noida, Bisrakh, Dadri, Jewar, Delhi, Ghaziabad, and the broader NCR region. Fill our application form or call us — our relationship manager will visit at a time convenient for you. We also review your documents beforehand to ensure everything is in perfect order before submission, avoiding delays.
How does the insurance claim process work?+
We assist clients through the entire claim process at no extra charge, even after policy purchase. For health insurance: cashless claims — just show your card at the network hospital TPA desk. Reimbursement claims — submit original bills within 15–30 days. For life insurance: we help families gather and submit all documents. For motor insurance: we coordinate with surveyors and follow up until settlement. Our support never ends.
