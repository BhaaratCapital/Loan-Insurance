FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

What is the minimum CIBIL score required for a home loan? +
Most lenders prefer a CIBIL score of 700 or above for home loans. However, we work with multiple NBFCs that can approve loans for scores between 650–699 with slightly higher rates. Contact us for a free CIBIL check.
How long does it take to get a home loan approved? +
Home loans typically take 7–10 working days from document submission to disbursement. Personal loans can be approved within 24 hours. We work with multiple lenders simultaneously to speed up your approval.
Is there any charge for your consultation service? +
Absolutely not. Our consultation is 100% free with no obligation whatsoever. We earn our fee from the lending institutions, not from you. You get expert guidance at zero cost.
What documents are needed to apply for a loan? +
Basic documents include: Identity Proof (Aadhaar / PAN / Passport), Address Proof (Aadhaar / Utility Bill), Income Proof (Salary Slips / Bank Statements / ITR), and property documents for home loans. Our team guides you through every step.
Do you offer doorstep service for document collection? +
Yes! We provide free doorstep document pickup service across Noida, Greater Noida, Bisrakh, and the entire NCR region. Our representative will come to your home or office at a time convenient for you.
Which banks and insurance companies are on your panel? +
We are partnered with 20+ institutions including SBI, HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Axis Bank, Bajaj Housing Finance, LIC Housing Finance, Hero Housing Finance, and many more. For insurance, we work with ICICI Lombard, Tata AIG, HDFC ERGO, Care Health, Niva Bupa, ICICI Prudential, Max Life, and Tata AIA.
