Mr. Chintu Singh

Co-Founder & Director — Bhaarat Capital (Est. 2024)

Mr. Chintu Singh is the driving force behind Bhaarat Capital. With deep expertise in financial products — from home loans to corporate insurance — he identified a critical gap: millions of families in Noida NCR were getting rejected, overcharged, or simply misguided when it came to loans and insurance.

His vision was bold yet simple — build a company that puts clients first, compares every available option, and delivers the best deal with zero hidden charges. Since founding Bhaarat Capital in 2024, he has personally helped 500+ families and businesses achieve their financial goals.

Under his leadership, Bhaarat Capital has grown to partner with 20+ banks, NBFCs, and 15+ insurance companies, making it one of Noida NCR's most trusted financial services firms.
