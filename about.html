Mr. Chintu Singh is the driving force behind Bhaarat Capital. With deep expertise in financial products — from home loans to corporate insurance — he identified a critical gap: millions of families in Noida NCR were getting rejected, overcharged, or simply misguided when it came to loans and insurance.
